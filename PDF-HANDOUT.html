Significance: Adherence ensures smooth payroll and standard workplace operations.
Success Metric: Boosts retention and motivation.
Challenges: Overburdening leadership teams.

- Structured onboarding aligns employees with goals.
- Legal compliance ensures regulatory adherence.
- Employee engagement fosters retention and motivation.
